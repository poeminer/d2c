Tier 1: < cLVL 25

▲

In the first tier, killing monsters that are more than 5 Levels below or above your cLVL will result in diminishing returns of XP awarded. Each additional Level is modified by a set variable divided by 256 to determine the % of the Experience you receive. This tier is designed to negatively impact the Player if the mLVL is significantly higher than the cLVL.
